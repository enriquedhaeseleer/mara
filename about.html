About MARA

Marathon Digital Holdings (MARA) is a leading Bitcoin mining company, operating one of the largest mining fleets with 57.3 EH/s as of April 2025. With 48,237 BTC in its treasury, MARA is the second-largest corporate Bitcoin holder. Committed to sustainable energy (52% per Cambridge report), MARA drives innovation in digital assets and has ventured into AI infrastructure and Kaspa mining.

Founded in 2010, MARA has grown to a market cap of $5.01B, with a focus on a 100% HODL strategy to maximize Bitcoin exposure. Its operations span Ohio, Texas, and beyond, leveraging advanced data centers.
